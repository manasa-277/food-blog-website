RAMYEON

If all this time you have always tasted Korean cuisine in the restaurant, now there is no harm in tasting korean cuisine in your own kitchen. You can try the korean Cuisine Recipe and serve it for families. Would have been nice! Good Luck !

Recipe

Ingredients

1. 1 pack of Korean Ramyeon

2. 1 egg

3. 1 stick of spring onions, cut thin

4. 1 sheet of kraft cheese

5. Additional side dishes: Korean rice cake (toppoki), crab stick or kimchi

Instruction

1.Cook ramyeon as usual.

2.Enter the chopped onion leaves and additional side dishes (if you want).

3.Enter the ramyeon seasoning. Add a little salt and pepper to strengthen the taste.

4.Place a sheet of kraft cheese on top of the boiling noodles.

5.Insert the eggs and turn off the stove, let the eggs cook in ramyeon hot sauce. If you don't like half-cooked eggs, put the eggs together with the leeks.

6.Let it be even worse, don't serve it in a bowl, just eat it directly from the pan.
