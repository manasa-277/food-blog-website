Recipe
Hello there, Ready for S'mores. So what are we waiting for, let's just get on with it.
Prep: 35min         Prep: 35min         Cook: 10min
Total: 4hr         Yield: Approx 2 dozen

1.   3 packages unflavored gelatin
Place the gelatin into the bowl of a stand mixer along with 1/2 cup of the water. Have the whisk attachment standing by.

2.   1 cup ice cold water, divided (50 g)
In a small saucepan combine the remaining 1/2 cup water, granulated sugar, corn syrup and salt. Place over medium high heat, cover and allow to cook for 3 to 4 minutes.

3.   12 ounces granulated sugar
Turn the mixer on low speed and, while running, slowly pour the sugar syrup down the side of the bowl into the gelatin mixture. Once you have added all of the syrup, increase the speed to high.

4.   1 cup light corn syrup
Combine the confectioners' sugar and cornstarch in a small bowl. Lightly spray a 13 by 9-inch metal baking pan with nonstick cooking spray.

5.   Half a teaspoon kosher salt
When ready, pour the mixture into the prepared pan, using a lightly oiled spatula for spreading evenly into the pan.

6.   1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Turn the marshmallows out onto a cutting board and cut into 1-inch squares using a pizza wheel dusted with the confectioners' sugar mixture.

7.   Half a cup confectioners' sugar
Combine the confectioners' sugar and cornstarch in a small bowl. Scoop the mixture into a piping bag fitted with a 1/2-inch round piping tip.

8.   Half a cup cornstarch
Let the macarons sit at room temperature for 30 minutes to 1 hour, until dry to the touch. Repeat.

9.   Nonstick spray
Pipe the mixture onto the prepared sheet pans lengthwise, leaving about 1-inch between each strip. Sprinkle the tops with enough of the remaining cornstarch and sugar mixture to lightly cover.

10.   Mixture
Cut into 1/2 inch pieces using a pizza wheel or scissors dusted with the confectioners' sugar mixture. Once cut, lightly dust all sides of each marshmallow with the remaining sugar mixture and store in an airtight container for up to a week.

11.   Last touch
Add the vanilla during the last minute of whipping. While the mixture is whipping prepare the pans as follows.
