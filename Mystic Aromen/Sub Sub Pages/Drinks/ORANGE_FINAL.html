Orange Crush Mocktail



This is a time to indulge in some deliciously chilled drinks and what can be better than the classic Orange Crush Mocktail recipe!
Here's how you can make this wonderful drink at home.




INGREDIENTS RECIPE










Step 1:-
To begin making Orange Mocktail, firstly mix the orange juice with lime/lemon juice in a mixing bowl.

Step 2:-
Take two glasses and put some ice cubes in it. Pour the juice mixture in the glass till it is half full. Pour some sprite or 7up in the same glass and a pinch of salt.

Step 3:-
Crush some mint leaves from hand and add in the juice. Add some more ice cubes if needed.

Step 4:-
Serve Orange Mocktail along with hot, delicious snacks for your house parties.

Step 5:-
Yo can add a spirg of rosemary to each glass. It adds a nice flavour to the orange mocktail.
