Mango Cooler



This super easy Mango Cocktail is perfect for quick mixing on any summer night.
Enjoy it on the porch, with friends, and at any gathering.




INGREDIENTS RECIPE










Step 1:-
To begin making Mango Cooler,a 1-quart glass measure, combine nectar, lemon juice, and sugar. Stir well to combine.

Step 2:-
Combine first four ingredients in a cocktail shaker and shake well to combine.

Step 3:-
Crush some mint leaves from hand and add in the juice. Add some more ice cubes if needed.

Step 4:-
Fill two tumblers or glasses with desired amount of ice. Pour cocktail into glasses. Pour 1 oz of lemon lime soda into each glass and stir.

Step 5:-
If desired, stir in diced mangoes. Serve cold.
